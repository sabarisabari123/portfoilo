About Us

Welcome to Khadir Mohideen College. Khadir Mohideen College is one of the oldest Minority colleges in Tamil Nadu, established in the year of 1955 as an affiliated college to the University of Madras and then affiliated to Bharathidasan University, Tiruchirappalli. The College was re-accredited (Second cycle) with 'B' grade by NAAC in 2011. We are dedicated to providing quality education and fostering holistic development in our students.
